I'm a second-year PhD researcher at Department of Anthropology at the University of California, Irvine.

My current research examines the digital data and infrastructure that communities need to effectively address entangled economic, environmental and public health challenges. I'm a member of the design group for the Platform for Experimental Collaborative Ethnography (PECE). You can sign up for a monthly PECE newsletter here).

I further co-develop a series of collaborative research, infrastructure and pedagogy projects.
